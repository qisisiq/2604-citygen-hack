# Vertical City Generator Overview

## Goal

Build a walkable, vertically organized, cylindrical sci-fi city generator in
Unity. The city should feel historically layered like Paris or Tokyo, but in a
tower-city form.

## Core Rule

Do not begin with meshes. Generate a semantic city graph first.

## The City Should Express

- layered history
- legible districts
- public and hidden circulation
- functional infrastructure
- social hierarchy
- macro-scale readability
- local-scale walkability

## Prototype Priorities

- coherence over randomness
- walkability over abstract correctness
- strong district identity
- clear navigation
- vertical drama
- believable support systems

## Avoid

- random skyscraper placement
- generic cyberpunk blocks
- flat-grid-only logic
- pure aesthetic generation without support systems
- overcomplicated simulation before the graph works
