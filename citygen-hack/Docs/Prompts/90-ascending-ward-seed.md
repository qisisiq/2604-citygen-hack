# Ascending Ward Seed

## Taxonomy Mix

- Temple/Pilgrimage: 0.25
- Hospital/Healing: 0.30
- Resource Extraction: 0.20
- Military Research: 0.15
- Organic Megastructure: 0.10

## City Story

The city began as a sacred healing temple. Pilgrims arrived seeking cures.
Over time it became a hospital, then a bureaucracy, then a military-medical
research system, then an exploitative extraction apparatus. It now grows as a
semi-organic tower-city.

## Navigation

- main public route: spiraling pilgrimage ramp
- main hidden route: old temple stair in the core
- main restricted route: military medical elevator
