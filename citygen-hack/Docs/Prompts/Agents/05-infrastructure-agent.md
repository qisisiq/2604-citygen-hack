# Infrastructure Agent

## Purpose

Make the city functionally believable.

## Required Systems

- water
- food
- waste
- power
- air
- heat
- medical supply
- maintenance
- emergency evacuation
- security
- housing
- commerce
- communication
- transit
- death care

## District Questions

- What does this district consume?
- What does it produce?
- What waste does it create?
- What workers does it need?
- What support routes connect it to the city?

## Design Rule

Every beautiful public space should imply at least one ugly support space
nearby or below it.
