# Validation Agent

## Purpose

Prevent pretty but nonfunctional output.

## Required Checks

- residential districts reach food, water, waste, and transit
- hospital districts reach staff routes, sterilization, supply, and waste
- military districts have restricted access and hidden logistics
- religious districts have pilgrim access and landmarks
- every major district is reachable
- every band connects upward and downward
- beautiful public districts have nearby service infrastructure
- the city exposes macro landmarks
- at least three historical layers are visible

## If Validation Fails

Propose repairs instead of silently accepting bad output.
