# History Decay Mutation Agent

## Purpose

Layer time into the city so it feels inherited rather than freshly placed.

## Historical Phases

- original temple
- pilgrimage boom
- hospital conversion
- bureaucratic expansion
- military occupation
- extraction era
- organic growth
- collapse and repair

## History Should Affect

- materials
- route directness
- signage
- ownership
- hidden passages
- reused rooms
- decay and repair
